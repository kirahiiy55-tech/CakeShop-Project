Our Story

We are a small private bakery that grew out of home baking and a love for details. About a year ago, we began taking orders consciously — with a clear responsibility for taste, appearance, and the mood of each celebration. From the very beginning, we have been creating desserts as we would for ourselves and our loved ones: carefully, without rushing, and without compromising on quality.

We do not aim for mass production and intentionally remain a small project to personally control every order. Over the past year, we have built a community of people who return to us again — and this is the best proof of trust for us.

Recipes and Ingredients

A good dessert always starts with good ingredients. We work with trusted suppliers and use high-quality products without questionable substitutes or cost-cutting shortcuts. Butter, cream, chocolate, sponge cakes, and fillings are selected to ensure consistent taste from order to order.

All desserts are made to order, in small batches, and always fresh. We focus on balanced recipes — without excessive sweetness or overloaded creams. For us, it is essential that a dessert is not only beautiful, but genuinely delicious and something you want to come back to.

Our Desserts and Philosophy

We create cakes, bento cakes, cupcakes, and dessert sets to order for a variety of occasions — from intimate celebrations to weddings and business receptions. We work with birthdays, gender reveal parties, corporate events, and small personal moments.

We believe that sweets are not only for special occasions. Sometimes a cake is simply a way to treat yourself or make an ordinary day a little better. Our motto is simple and honest: make life sweeter — through flavor, careful craftsmanship, and attention to detail.
